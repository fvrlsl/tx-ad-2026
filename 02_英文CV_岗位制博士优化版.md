### 💼 Selected Research Experience & Projects

**Project 1 — CuIn₂S₄ Nanozyme for Biosensing  *(2023.09 – 2024.10)***
- Designed and synthesized CuIn₂S₄ nanozyme exhibiting tunable peroxidase-mimetic activity (turnover ~ 5× horseradish peroxidase under optimal conditions).
- Built a colorimetric biosensor with detection limit at nanomolar range for H₂O₂-related analytes.
- Performed Gaussian DFT calculations to elucidate the H₂O₂ activation pathway and Cu-S electron-transfer mechanism.
- *Outcome: First-author publication in Chemical Engineering Journal (IF 13.3).*

**Project 2 — Dynamic Covalent Hyaluronic Acid Hydrogels  *(2024.01 – 2024.08)***
- Synthesized oxidized hyaluronic acid (OHA) and crosslinked with adipic dihydrazide to form self-healing dynamic covalent hydrogels.
- Demonstrated injectability, shear-thinning behavior, and pH-responsive degradation suitable for regenerative-medicine applications.
- *Outcome: First-author publication in International Journal of Biological Macromolecules (IF 8.2).*

**Project 3 — English Research & Teaching Assistance to Dr. Alireza Asem  *(2023.09 – present)***
- Co-translated and co-authored multiple international manuscripts and conference submissions.
- Served as primary point of contact for English-language correspondence and proofreading within the lab.
- Coordinated visiting-scholar logistics and bilingual lab seminars.
